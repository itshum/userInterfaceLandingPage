Why Farm Fresh?

We are dedicated to ending the cycle of food waste.

We care about what you’re eating. Our produce is always local and quadruple checked to make sure it hasn’t been treated with synthetic pesticides or herbicides.

We are on a mission to connect consumers to their local farmers.

We believe in giving the power back to the smaller farmers. At Farm Fresh farmers completely manage their own inventory, prices, and retain their own personal customer database.
